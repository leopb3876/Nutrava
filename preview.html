Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

What makes Nutrava different from other skincare brands?+
Most skincare is a lottery — you buy what the aisle has. Nutrava matches a routine to your skin type, top concern, sensitivity, and current habits. Every formula is clinically dosed, fragrance-free where it matters, and transparent about what’s inside.
How does the personalised quiz work?+
Our quiz takes under 60 seconds and asks about your skin type, top concern, current routine, and sensitivity. It branches based on your answers and matches you with a 2–4 product routine built specifically for your skin.
How long until I see results?+
Hydration and calming products usually feel different on day one. For brightening and anti-aging actives, expect visible improvement in 2–4 weeks of consistent use, with full benefits building over 6–8 weeks.
Can I use these products together?+
Yes — every routine is designed to layer. We handle the order and balance so you're not mixing incompatible actives. A good rule: apply thinnest to thickest, water-based serums before oils, SPF last in the morning.
What's your shipping and returns policy?+
We ship to the US only. Orders are fulfilled by our US-based fulfillment partner and typically arrive within 5–10 business days. Free shipping on orders over $50. Every product comes with our 30-day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy, we'll refund you in full, no questions asked.
Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?+
The majority of our products are vegan and gluten-free. A few products (like Omega-3 Fish Oil) are sourced from fish, but we always offer plant-based alternatives. Every product page clearly lists all ingredients, allergens, and dietary information so you know exactly what you're taking.
