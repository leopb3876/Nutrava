Common Questions

How does the personalised quiz work? +

Our quiz takes about 60 seconds. It branches based on your answers — your skin type, top concern, current routine, and sensitivity — and matches you with a 2–4 product routine engineered for your skin profile.

How long until I see results? +

Hydration and calming products feel different on day one. Brightening and anti-aging actives need 2–4 weeks of consistent use, with full benefits compounding over 6–8 weeks.

Are your formulas vegan and cruelty-free? +

100% cruelty-free, never tested on animals, and the vast majority are vegan. Every product page lists the full INCI ingredient list so you can check for anything you avoid.
